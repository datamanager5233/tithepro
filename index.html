Mobile Money

Transactions from the designated church mobile number

📱 How Mobile Money Integration Works

1
Church members send Mobile Money to the designated church number with their tithe number in the message (e.g. "TBGB0042 Tithe")
2
The Google Apps Script runs every 5 minutes to parse incoming SMS from a linked Google Sheet (populated via Android SMS Forwarder app)
3
Transactions are automatically matched to members and recorded in the MobileMoney sheet tab
4
A thank-you SMS is sent back to the member via the script confirming receipt

Required Setup

  • Install SMS Forwarder app on the church phone
  • Configure it to forward SMS to the Google Sheet webhook URL
  • Google Script handles parsing, matching & notifications
